Free TAFE courses

Get the skills you need without the Tuition fees! Any Australian citizen, Australian permanent resident or New Zealand citizen living in Victoria can now enrol in a Free TAFE course or government-subsidised training regardless of their prior skills and qualifications. Previous qualification: You can do your first Free TAFE course no matter what qualifications you already hold. This applies to all qualifications - whether they be through TAFE or university. This is because the 'upskilling rule' has been removed. MurrayTAFE has expanded the Free TAFE courses in 2024 to include Certificate III in Commerical Cookery (SIT30821). 2015

International students

For over a decade, MurrayTAFE has been a welcoming destination for international students seeking to study in Mildura. We provide a wide range of career options with practical qualifications in fields such as Automotive, Horticulture, Aged Care, Early Childhood Education, Cookery and Hospitality. To enrol in our Certificate, Diploma, and Advanced Diploma programs, a minimum English language proficiency test result of 6.0 IELTS Academic or 50 PTE Academic (or equivalent) is required. However, we also offer Intensive English Language courses (ELICOS) to help students achieve the necessary language skills for their chosen career path. Additionally, some of our MurrayTAFE courses can be packaged with a Bachelor's degree program at a partner university. This means that students may earn university credits for completing recognised courses at MurrayTAFE.

New course - Protected Horticulture

Protected Horticulture covers a range of horticultural sectors such as commercial hydroponics, greenhouse facilities and covered crops. Certificate II in Protected Horticulture provides the skills and knowledge required for individuals to work in support roles in areas of picking, packing, crop and nursery work.

2011

Apprenticeship Training at SuniTAFE

MurrayTAFE can give you the practical skills and knowledge you need for your career. An apprenticeship or traineeship is a partnership between you, your employer and MurrayTAFE. Your employer will provide you real workplace experience and teach you skills on-the-job while MurrayTAFE provides formal training. If you’re still at school you can undertake a school-based apprenticeship or traineeship while completing VCE or VCAL. Apprenticeship, Traineeship and Pre-Apprenticeship courses at MurrayTAFE are available across a range of industry and trade areas. An apprenticeship or traineeship gives you the chance to build transferable skills that attract employers, making you more job-ready. Apprenticeships and traineeships can commence at any time. Every apprentice and trainee must have a business willing to employ them and a Training Contract, which is organised through a local Australian Apprenticeship Support Network (AASN) provider. MurrayTAFE has great industry partners and access to some of the latest technology to teach into the future.
